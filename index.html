Scientific Methodology

The ESP32 Central Hub collects ultrasonic, gas, LDR, and camera data. YOLO detection identifies cars and trucks. Barriers and lanes adjust automatically.

Key Components:

  • ESP32 Central Hub
  • ESP32 CAM
  • MQ135 Gas Sensor
  • Ultrasonic Sensors
  • LDR Sensors
  • SG90 Servo Motors
  • LCD Display
